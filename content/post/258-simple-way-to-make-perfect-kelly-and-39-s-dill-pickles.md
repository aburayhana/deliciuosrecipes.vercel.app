1. Get 6 clove fresh garlic
1. Get 1 3/4 bunch fresh basil/ 1-2 leaves per jar
1. Take 1 bunch fresh oregano/ 1 small piece per jar
1. Prepare 6 small red or yellow cayenne peppers or banana peppers
